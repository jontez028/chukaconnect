How Payments Work

A simple guide to all payment methods on ChukaConnect.

📱
M-Pesa STK Push

The most common method. You enter your phone number and an automatic prompt is sent to your phone. Enter your PIN to confirm. Payment reflects instantly.

Instant · Recommended
📡
Airtel Money

For Airtel subscribers. You receive a USSD prompt on your phone to confirm payment. Can also pay manually via *334#.

Instant · Airtel numbers
🏦
Bank Transfer

For larger payments like hostel deposits. Transfer from any Kenyan bank or mobile banking app. Takes 1-3 business days.

1-3 days · Large amounts
📱
QR Code (Riders)

Boda riders generate a personal QR code. Customers scan it with M-Pesa app to pay instantly. No cash needed.

Instant · No cash needed
